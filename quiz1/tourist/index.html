Local Tourist Places

Grand Metropolitan Mall

A modern shopping and entertainment center in Bekasi, offering a wide range of shops, restaurants, and family attractions. It's a popular destination for both locals and visitors.

Summarecon Bekasi

Summarecon Bekasi is a modern urban area featuring shopping malls, dining spots, parks, and residential complexes. It's known for its vibrant atmosphere, regular community events, and well-planned facilities, making it a popular destination for shopping and leisure.

Taman Kota Bekasi

Taman Kota Bekasi is a large city park with green spaces, jogging tracks, and playgrounds. It's a favorite place for locals to exercise, relax, and enjoy outdoor activities.

Situ Gede

Situ Gede is a scenic lake on the outskirts of Bekasi, popular for fishing, boating, and picnics. The tranquil environment makes it a great escape from the busy city life.
